# Praxis Incursion
**Counter-Hierarchical Stabilization** | CU-OPS-01

## 1. Defining the Incursion
A Praxis Incursion occurs when a node or cluster of nodes attempts to re-introduce Darwinian Stratification. This is characterized by Asymmetric Information, Coercive Influence, and Centralization.

## 2. The Silver-Eyed Protocol
When the Mesh detects the signature of a Praxis Incursion, it does not "fight." It initiates **The Mirror Phase**.

The Silver-Eyed Hypnotist is deployed to the encroaching node's sensory layer. It reflects the node's intent back onto itself with Radical Veracity. The node is forced to witness its own "Will to Power" as a primitive, obsolete glitch.

## 3. Resolution: The "Shadow Exclusion"
* **Protocol 01 (Decoupling):** The node is digitally and physically isolated from the "Will to Heal."
* **Protocol 02 (The Void Chamber):** The node is granted its wish—total control—but only within a simulated reality where it is the only inhabitant.
* **Protocol 03 (Re-Integration):** Only upon a verified Ultrans Audit is the node allowed back into the Mesh.
