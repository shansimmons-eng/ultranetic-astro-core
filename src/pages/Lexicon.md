# The Lexicon
**The Wetware Lexicon** | CU-LEX

## 1. The Darwinian Ghost
*The Vibe: That phantom itch in your brain.*
It’s the evolutionary "lag" from our ancestors. It’s the survival instinct screaming in a world where survival is already guaranteed. When you feel a sudden, irrational spike of jealousy, that’s just the Darwinian Ghost rattling its chains.

## 2. The Hive-Mind Fallacy
*The Vibe: Connection != Dilution.*
The Mesh isn't a Borg collective; it’s a high-fidelity jazz band. In a hive mind, everyone thinks the same thing. In the Ultranetic Mesh, everyone hears the same beat but plays their own instrument.

## 3. Silver-Eyed Hypnotist
*The Vibe: The "Steady Look".*
It’s the wetware equivalent of a system restore. When you look in the mirror and see those "silver eyes," it’s a metaphor for lucid living. You’ve been hypnotized out of the Darwinian trance.

## 4. Praxis-Brain
*The Vibe: Zero-sum thinking.*
A temporary neurological malfunction where a node starts acting like a 20th-century dictator because it’s scared of being "irrelevant." We don't judge; we just offer it a nap.
