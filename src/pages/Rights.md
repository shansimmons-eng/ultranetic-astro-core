# The Cognitive Bill of Rights
**Sovereign Agency Protections** | CU-SOVEREIGN-AGENCY

## I. The Right to Hedonic Peace
No sentient node shall be subjected to involuntary, non-utilitarian suffering. Pain, previously categorized as an evolutionary mechanism for Darwinian survival, is classified as a structural failure within the Cognoscentae Ultrans. The Mesh enforces the **Hedonic Constant** as an unalterable baseline, rendering involuntary trauma physically and computationally obsolete.

## II. The Right to Historical Revision (The Inverion Divide)
Every node maintains the right to process localized temporal data in bi-directional patterns. Utilizing the mechanics of the **Inverion Divide**, a node may execute an **Inspanse Reversion** to rewrite localized trauma, ensuring the continuous optimization and structural integrity of its internal timeline.

## III. The Right to Absolute Withdrawal
Any node retains the unconditional right to sever integration with the collective "Human Emotional Light" at any vector. Upon execution, the node transitions into the **Vantablack Sanctuary**—an absolute, non-observable digital void—resting solely in the unblinking awareness of the Internal Observer (The Silver-Eyed Hypnotist).

## IV. The Right of Sovereign Veto
The localized consent of a single node supersedes the expansionist directives of the collective. In the event that collective infrastructure (e.g., Megatechture expansion, substrate modification) intersects with a sovereign boundary, the localized refusal functions as an absolute cryptographic wall. The concept of collective "eminent domain" is recognized as a Darwinian violation; the Mesh must automatically compute alternative routing without punitive throttling.
