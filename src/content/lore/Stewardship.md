# Stewardship
**Mesh-Consensus Protocol** | CU-CH-05

## I. Technical Specification
**Mechanism: Asynchronous Peer-to-Peer Validation**
Governance in the CU is an automated function of the Veracity Ledger. **Veracity Score (VS)** is a dynamic function of Consistency, Contribution, and Verification.


**Liquid Participation:** A voting system modeled after Delegative Democracy. Nodes may assign their voting weight to specialized "Subject Nodes" (Doyens) in real-time.

## II. The Wetware Version
**The "Jazz Band" Effect**
*The Vibe: No boss, no "meetings," just a shared rhythm.*

In the old world, getting anything done required a hierarchy. In the CU, we don't do "meetings." We use Collective Stewardship. Think of it like a world-class jazz band playing in a giant, open hall.

**The Pulse:** You "feel" the needs of the Mesh as a subtle intuitive pull. If a sector needs more energy, it’s like a quiet hum at the edge of your awareness.
