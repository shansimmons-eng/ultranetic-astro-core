The OI (Organic Intelligence) Matrix represents the pinnacle of hybrid computing. By integrating living biological neural tissue clusters with glowing gold circuitry, the system achieves unprecedented parallel processing capabilities. Suspended in a translucent nutrient gel under clinical lighting, the matrix requires constant biological upkeep but delivers computational power that dwarfs traditional silicon-based architectures.
