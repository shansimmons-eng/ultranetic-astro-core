Operating at a massive brutalist industrial scale, the Gyrotron is a deep geothermal wellhead that utilizes directed energy rather than physical drill bits. A glowing blue-white energy beam penetrates the deep borehole, instantly vaporizing rock to reach the planet's mantle. This allows for the rapid deployment of geothermal taps in previously impenetrable geological formations, securing near-infinite baseload power.
