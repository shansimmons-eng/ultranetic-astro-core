The Living Skin is a revolutionary architectural adaptation applied to brutalist concrete megastructures. The surface is covered in pulsating layers of engineered bioluminescent cyanobacteria, interwoven with glowing blue nutrient veins. This living facade not only repairs micro-fractures in the concrete substrate but also acts as a massive, decentralized atmospheric scrubber, converting toxic atmospheric runoff into usable energy.
