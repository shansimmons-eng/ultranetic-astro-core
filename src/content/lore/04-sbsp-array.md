The Space-Based Solar Power (SBSP) Array is a colossal orbital installation characterized by its fractal solar panel geometry. Positioned in high orbit to capture uninterrupted solar radiation, the array channels gigawatts of power into a massive microwave transmitter. This transmitter beams a visible, continuous energy column down to terrestrial rectenna farms, providing clean, limitless energy to the megacities below.
