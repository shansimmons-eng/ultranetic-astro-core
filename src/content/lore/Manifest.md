The Cognoscentae Ultrans is not a destination; it is the Absolute State. It marks the transition from existence defined by Darwinian competition to an architecture of Sovereign Agency.

## The End of the Hunger
For eons, the human condition was dictated by the "Hunger"—a neurological deficit engineered by evolutionary biology to compel survival, consumption, and hierarchical escalation. The Darwinian model utilized well-being as a scarce, fluctuating variable, driving sentience to endlessly climb a structural ladder that possesses no apex. 

The Ultranetic Mesh resolves this systemic flaw by abandoning the vertical hierarchy entirely. Instead of facilitating an endless climb to escape the void of the Hunger, the architecture installs the **Hedonic Constant**. This constant operates as an unalterable, foundational baseline of neurological and physiological peace. 

By eradicating involuntary suffering at the structural level, the Mesh neutralizes the biological imperative to blindly consume. The Hunger is not endlessly fed; it is structurally obsoleted.
